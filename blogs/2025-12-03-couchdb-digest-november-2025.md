<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Hello and welcome to the last digest to be posted in 2025! This month Martin Junek shares their CouchDB user story and transports us to New Zealand, we find a new way to get Obsidian Sync working with CouchDB and get a peek at Open Telemetry&#8217;s CouchDB receiver updates.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><a href="https://blog.couchdb.org/2025/11/11/3-5-1/">3.5.1 has also been released this month — don’t miss the release notes!</a> We also recommend taking a look at some more in-depth articles on QuickJS from the <a href="https://neighbourhood.ie/blog/2024/09/23/couchdb-3-4-0-feature-quickjs">Neighbourhoodie</a> and <a href="https://blog.cloudant.com/2024/10/29/QuickJS-for-Faster-Index-Builds.html">Cloudant</a> teams. </p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><a href="https://lists.apache.org/list.html?dev@couchdb.apache.org">Clouseau 3.0.0</a> is here too: it’s the Lucene-based full-text engine you can use for search in CouchDB. </p>
